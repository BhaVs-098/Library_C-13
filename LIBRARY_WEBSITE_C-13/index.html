The Library of Vishnu Institute of Technology was built to keep up International Standards, is located in separate block - a new multi-storied building with carpet area of 1878 sq. meters. with sufficient area for stacks and reading space. It is fully computerized and furnished, containing a large number of volumes, titles, journals, periodicals and newspapers. With the ambience provided by the facilities and atmosphere, it can be truly called a 'Knowledge Centre' and also a 'Learning Centre' and all the students and staff find it conducive for constructive study.
